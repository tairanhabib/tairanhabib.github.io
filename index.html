about

I'm a first-generation undergraduate in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at MIT. My work sits where hardware meets code — soft robotics, control systems, and embedded engineering — and it's grown out of years of building real things, from load-tested strongman equipment to environmental sensors to a pneumatic robot designed to grasp delicate objects.

The throughline across everything is the same: take a physical problem, build the system that solves it, and test it until it holds. Right now that means autonomous control for a loop-closing vine robot and bridging computer vision with motion.
